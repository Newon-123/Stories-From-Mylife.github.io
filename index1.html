The Heart-warming Weather in Shimla Hills

It was about the end of , my vacations in Shimla I have to leave this place day-after tomorrow to my Home Town. I was staying in a inn , near the hill station. I have made a few friends by living in the inn and the skinning area, one of my best friend was Riya she was an young hot beauty with long haired girl. She lived near my Home Town, a few miles away. I had spend the happiest and the most enjoyable moments of my life in Shimla Hills. The day when I woke up, the weather was not too much hot, nor cold it was an Heart Warming Weather in Shimla hills birds where charping from the hills which echoed in the hill station and I said ‘Goodbye to all my lovely friends and an warm hug’. It was the time for me to leave the place and wait for the next winter to come. When I reached my Home Town I arrived at my home kept my belongings, asked the Rikshawale bhaiya to take me to Riya’s house. When I reached there I gave her a long hug and sipped up some hot chocolate up, said her ‘ It is getting dark I think, I may leave before the dog starts barking at me’ ‘Goodbye’ I said and she too...
Here the winter vacations ended and I’m waiting for the next one upcoming, very next year.
