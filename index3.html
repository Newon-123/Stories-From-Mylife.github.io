The Knocking Wind Through My Window

The night when I was sitting, in my room with my laptop and it was drizzling outside and I liked to keep my window open, so that I can smell, feel the drizzling rain fall on my face.
But
When I was finished my coding work I got my book, an warm water bottle (beside my bed on the side table), blanket on and it started raining at very high rate and I heard, Boobacha said the girl I sneaked out the window and saw her their getting, wet in the rain of the night sky I called her 'kaun hai' and she looked at me and said 'may I come in your house for this dark night', I thinked about it and replied in few seconds'. 'ohh! Yes you can come in wait there I will come to take you in' and I brought her in my house, ( although I was all alone in my house that night ) I told her to feel relax here I gave her a towel and a hot cup of coffee. 'Thank you very much, for giving me an shelter' she said, I asked her'.what is you 'Name young beauty' she said her name was aditi, I said 'ohh! Aditi, where do you live' she replied 'I live about 11 miles away from this town' I ignored to ask her for what work she came in this town. I offered her to sleep with me and I told her to go to the upper first room of mine and sleep I will sleep in the hall. The next morning I got up very early in the morning about 6:00 am I saw her in kitchen when I went there to get some milk she told me that she will be leaving in 2 hours. I talked to her and got to known her address in some few talks with her and she gave me a scarf of her's I kept it as an thank you gift and before leaving she invited me to her house to stay their for some days. Few months passed I went to the address given by her and there was no one expect an old women to whom I asked about Aditi, what I heard about her was just scary that "She passed away a few years ago" and the night when the it rains heavily she fools men and I was one of them.
